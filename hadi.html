My project

Developing strategies for wind turbine control to enhance the energy efficiency

This research focuses on developing advanced control strategies for wind turbines based on artificial intelligence. Through machine learning algorithms, operating data is analyzed and wind speed changes are predicted to achieve real-time adaptive adjustment of parameters such as blade pitch and yaw. The focus is on solving the problem of optimizing power generation efficiency under variable wind speed conditions, with the goals of maximizing power output and reducing mechanical loads.
"A high-fidelity digital twin model is established to accurately restore the dynamic characteristics of the unit through virtual simulation. Combined with IoT sensor data, real-time evaluation of equipment health status and fault warning are achieved. The main goal is to extend the life of key components (such as main bearings and gearboxes) and improve system reliability, while providing a verification platform for control strategies.
